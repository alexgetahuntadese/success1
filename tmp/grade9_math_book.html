Let's see chapters by chapter breakdown:

Unit 1: Further on Sets

This unit helps students understand the structure and classification of sets, including elements, operations, and real-life applications involving set relationships.

Unit 2: The Number System

Students will deepen their understanding of number classifications, including rational, irrational, and real numbers, while revisiting operations with integers and fractions.

Unit 3: Solving Equations

Students develop techniques to solve both linear and non-linear equations, apply algebraic rules, and interpret their solutions in real-world problem contexts.

Unit 4: Solving Inequalities

Through this unit, students become familiar with solving and graphing different types of inequalities including linear, absolute value, and quadratic forms.

Unit 5: Introduction to Trigonometry

The basics of trigonometric ratios are introduced, allowing students to explore relationships in right-angled triangles and apply trigonometry to geometry problems.

Unit 6: Regular Polygons

This unit explores properties of polygons, enabling learners to calculate interior and exterior angles and recognize patterns in regular geometric shapes.

Unit 7: Congruency and Similarity

Students learn to identify congruent and similar figures, use geometric theorems, and apply ratios of sides and areas to solve comparative shape problems.

Unit 8: Vectors in Two Dimensions

This unit introduces vector and scalar quantities, vector representation, and operations, helping students solve problems involving direction and magnitude.

Unit 9: Statistics and Probability

Students are introduced to data collection, interpretation, and probability, helping them make informed predictions and analyze statistical patterns.
