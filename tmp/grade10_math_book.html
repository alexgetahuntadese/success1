Ethiopian New Curriculum Grade 10 Mathematics Student Textbook

The New Curriculum Grade 10 Mathematics student textbook is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of various mathematical concepts and their applications. The textbook covers topics such as relations and functions, polynomial functions, exponential and logarithmic functions, trigonometric functions, circles, solid figures, and coordinate geometry. The textbook aims to equip students with the necessary mathematical knowledge and skills to excel in their academic and professional pursuits.

Chapters Overview

-

The New Curriculum Grade 10 Mathematics Student Textbook contains 7 units, they are:

  • Unit 1: Relations and Functions
  • Unit 2: Polynomial Functions
  • Unit 3: Exponential and Logarithmic Functions
  • Unit 4: Trigonometric Functions
  • Unit 5: Circles
  • Unit 6: Solid Figures
  • Unit 7: Coordinate Geometry

Unit 1: Relations and Functions

  • Introduces the concepts of relations and functions, including types of functions and their properties.
  • Covers the Cartesian coordinate system and graphing of relations and functions.

Unit 2: Polynomial Functions

  • Defines polynomial functions and explores their properties, operations, and graphs.
  • Discusses the zeros and applications of polynomial functions.

Unit 3: Exponential and Logarithmic Functions

  • Introduces exponents and logarithms, and their properties.
  • Explores the graphs and applications of exponential and logarithmic functions.
  • Covers the relationship between exponential and logarithmic functions.

Unit 4: Trigonometric Functions

  • Introduces radian measure and the basic trigonometric functions.
  • Explores trigonometric identities, equations, and applications.

Unit 5: Circles

  • Examines the symmetrical and angle properties of circles.
  • Covers arc length, perimeters, and areas of circle segments and sectors.

Unit 6: Solid Figures

  • Revises concepts of cylinders and prisms.
  • Introduces pyramids, cones, spheres, and their properties.
  • Explores the surface areas and volumes of composed solids.

Unit 7: Coordinate Geometry

  • Covers distance between two points, division of a line segment, and the equation of a line.
  • Discusses slopes of parallel and perpendicular lines, and the equation of a circle.

Learning Outcome

-

Students will gain understanding in:

  • Relations and Functions: Understanding the concepts of relations and functions, and their properties.
  • Graphing and Analyzing Functions: Developing skills in graphing and analyzing various types of functions.
  • Mathematical Problem Solving: Applying mathematical principles to solve problems involving polynomial, exponential, logarithmic, and trigonometric functions.
  • Circle Properties: Gaining knowledge of circle properties and their applications.
  • Solid Figures: Exploring the characteristics and calculations of solid figures.
  • Coordinate Geometry: Applying coordinate geometry principles to solve real-world problems.
