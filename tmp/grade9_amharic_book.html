Learning Outcome

-

Students will gain understanding in:

  • Enhancing Language Skills in Amharic: Understanding the development of listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills in the Amharic language to enhance overall communication abilities.
  • Literary and Linguistic Concepts: Gaining knowledge of various literary and linguistic concepts, including poetry, drama, and grammar, to foster a deeper appreciation for the structure and beauty of language.
  • Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving: Developing critical thinking skills and the ability to solve problems effectively, helping students evaluate situations and make informed decisions.
  • Effective Communication: Strengthening both written and verbal communication skills to enable students to express their ideas clearly and persuasively in various contexts.
  • Awareness of Social, Economic, and Environmental Issues: Understanding important social, economic, and environmental issues, empowering students to think critically about global challenges and their role in society.
